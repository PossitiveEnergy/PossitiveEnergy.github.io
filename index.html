Welcome!

Thank you for taking the time to visit our community.

My name is Jayson, and I run a nonprofit organization in District 69 dedicated to bringing positivity and support to our local community. Our mission is to help address homelessness by offering assistance, organizing cleanups, and responsibly removing waste from highways, trails, parks, and other public spaces.

We strive to make District 69 a safer, cleaner, and more welcoming place for everyone to enjoy.

Feel free to explore our Gallery page to see our work in action, or visit the Contact page to get involved. You can also follow us on Facebook and our other social media platforms. We welcome helping hands and donations.

I look forward to connecting with you.

~ Jayson Perry
